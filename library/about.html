About....

Library management system software for monitoring and controlling the transaction in library. It mainly focus on basic operations in library like adding new books,updating information,searching books and numbers and facility to borrow and return books.It is designed to help users to maintain and organize a library. This software is easy to use for the advanced users.Book store is available for different categories of book.Students can able to search their book and can find it easily in the respective categories. Contact information can be given in order to issue the books and the particular notification will be sent to the student about the details of book.Students can issue their library cards mentioned with its due date and the informations.Updation of books will be given by the admins or librarians.Students can also read their books in online mentioned under various categories.Image slider is also available to get to know about books.Reviews can be given regardinf the works done under student side.Book store is available seperately to purchase books in online itself.
